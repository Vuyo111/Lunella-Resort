About Lunella Retreat

A dream realized. A sanctuary for the soul.

Our Story

Nestled in the serene Muldersdrift countryside, Lunella Retreat is a luxury spa and boutique resort born from years of dreaming, planning, and prayer. It all began with a vision — a five-star spa offering world-class wellness, beauty, and holistic healing, combined with elegant accommodation and curated dining.

Our founder's dream was to create a place where tranquility meets thoughtful design — from our spa’s beauty bar and hydrotherapy pool to the supervised kiddies lounge and serene restaurant garden. Every corner of Lunella Retreat is designed to bring you comfort, luxury, and peace.

Our Vision

To be the most serene and welcoming retreat in South Africa, offering guests a uniquely soulful and luxurious experience.

Our Location

You’ll find us tucked away on lush green grounds in Muldersdrift — a quiet escape just minutes from Johannesburg’s bustle, but miles away in spirit.
