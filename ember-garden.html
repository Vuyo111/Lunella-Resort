The Ember Garden

Outdoor dining reimagined with fire, flavor, and the stars above.

Dining Beneath the Stars

Nestled in a secluded part of the retreat, The Ember Garden offers a relaxed, elegant open-air dining space built around a modern fire pit centerpiece. Surrounded by soft lights and warm ambiance, guests can unwind with artisan meals, crackling firewood, and unforgettable sunsets.

Whether you're sipping a cocktail, enjoying an evening braai, or simply gazing at the night sky, this is your invitation to slow down and reconnect.
